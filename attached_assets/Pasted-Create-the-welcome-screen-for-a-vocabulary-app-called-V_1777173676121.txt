Create the welcome screen for a vocabulary app called "Verba". It's a premium app for studying GRE and English vocabulary. This is only the first screen — the welcome page the user sees when opening the app.
See the attached reference image for the exact visual style.
LAYOUT:

Fullscreen, vertically centered
Small circular "V" logo mark above the title (amber stroke, subtle amber glow)
App name "Verba" large and bold in the center, with a white-to-amber vertical gradient on the letters
Below: tagline "words that stick" in lowercase, smaller, light weight, letter-spaced
Below (with generous spacing): a "Start" button with amber-to-orange gradient, fully rounded pill shape
Background: pure black with GRE vocabulary words floating at low opacity in the space around the title (words like: luminous, ephemeral, cogent, liminal, reverie) — these are subtle decorative elements, amber color #D97706 with warm text-shadow glow
Two soft radial amber halos glowing from the center-top and center-bottom of the background

DESIGN:

Dark mode: background #0A0A0A, white text
Accent color (amber/orange): #D97706 for floating words and logo glow
Button gradient: #F59E0B → #EA580C
Title font: Space Grotesk (Google Fonts), bold weight 700, very large
Tagline and button font: Inter (Google Fonts), weight 300
Floating words font: Inter weight 300, small size, letter-spaced
Button: amber/orange gradient background, white text, fully rounded border-radius, generous padding, warm amber glow shadow
Style inspired by Apple Fitness (rounded elements, bold typography on black), Brilliant (educational premium feel), Pinterest (warmth, rounded corners)
Generous spacing everywhere — let the design breathe

ANIMATIONS (Framer Motion):

Title "Verba" appears with fade-in + slight slide up (0.3s, ease-out)
Tagline appears 0.15s after the title, same effect
Button appears 0.15s after the tagline, same effect
Floating words fade in slowly and drift very slightly (subtle, non-distracting)
Logo "V" has a pulsing amber glow (shadow breathes slowly, 3s loop)
Button has a pulsing warm glow on its amber shadow (2s loop)
On click the button scales to 0.95 for 0.1s (snappy press feedback)
Background radial halos pulse slowly (opacity 0.3 → 0.5, 4s loop)

TECH STACK:

React + Vite
Tailwind CSS
Framer Motion for all animations
Google Fonts: Space Grotesk (700) + Inter (300, 400)

FEELING:
Energetic, premium, confident. Like opening a luxury app that makes you want to start studying immediately. Warm amber tones against deep black create a sense of focused intensity.
